BioSimulator*



I worked with three others on this project which sought to connect pedestrians with their environment around them as to improve their general experience and bring benefit to congested and lifeless urban areas. BioSimulator's experience is tailor for enclosed urban thoroughfares such as tunnels and coves. The video presentation was shot and edited by myself.























Gridlock in Bangkok



I worked with two others on this project which presents the lack of urban planning that coincided with the city of Bangkok's economic up turn throughout the latter half of the 20th century. Through key statistics and visualisations it shows where the cities severe traffic and commuting congestion problems have originated. Video presentation edited by myself.























Wickr Man



This solo project was done as to improve my video editing ability, so the brief allowed free choice on the topic of the video itself. I chose to create this video which emphasises the importance of not only freedom of speech but also anonymity of speech as they serve as important functions when developing safe and sustainable societies.
